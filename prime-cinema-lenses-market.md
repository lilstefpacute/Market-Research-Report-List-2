<p><p>Prime lenses and zoom lenses are different types of cinema lenses used in the film industry. Prime lenses have a fixed focal length, which means they provide a consistent magnification and field of view. They are known for their superior image quality and wide maximum aperture, allowing for low-light shooting. On the other hand, zoom lenses offer a variable focal length, allowing cinematographers to change the magnification and field of view without changing the lens. While they may not match the image quality of prime lenses, zoom lenses offer flexibility and convenience in capturing different shots.</p></p>
