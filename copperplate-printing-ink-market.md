<p><p>Copperplate printing ink market encompasses three primary types: solvent-borne ink, water-borne ink, and oil-based ink. Solvent-borne inks are composed of solvents that evaporate during the drying process, resulting in a durable and vibrant color. Water-borne inks, on the other hand, use water as a base, making them less harmful to the environment and easier to clean. Lastly, oil-based inks utilize oils as a medium and provide good adhesion on various substrates, but may require longer drying times. These various types cater to diverse printing needs and offer different advantages and disadvantages.</p></p>
