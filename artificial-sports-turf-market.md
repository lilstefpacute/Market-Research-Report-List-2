Firstly, the increased focus on player safety has led sports organizations and facility owners to choose artificial turf over natural grass, as it offers better shock absorption and reduces the risk of injuries. Additionally, artificial turf requires less maintenance and is more cost-effective in the long run, as it does not need mowing, watering, or fertilizing. It also provides a consistent playing surface regardless of weather conditions, making it ideal for year-round use.</p><p>Moreover, the growing popularity of sports activities and the increasing number of sports tournaments and events worldwide are fueling the demand for artificial sports turf.
