<p><p>The optical fingerprint sensor market consists of two main types: Multispectral Imaging and Electro-optical Imaging. Multispectral Imaging utilizes multiple wavelengths of light to capture and analyze fingerprint images, enhancing accuracy and reliability even in challenging conditions like wet or dirty fingers. On the other hand, Electro-optical Imaging uses an array of tiny sensors to capture high-resolution fingerprint images, providing fast and accurate results. Both technologies improve security and authentication applications, ensuring greater convenience and efficiency in various industries such as banking, government, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
