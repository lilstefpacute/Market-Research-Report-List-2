<p><p>Capacitive accelerometer sensors are used to measure acceleration or vibration in various applications. These sensors can be categorized based on the number of axes they can measure: 1-Axis, 2-Axis, and 3-Axis. </p><p>1-Axis accelerometers can measure acceleration in only one direction, such as vertical or horizontal. </p><p>2-Axis accelerometers can measure acceleration in two perpendicular directions, allowing detection of both vertical and horizontal movements. </p><p>3-Axis accelerometers offer the highest level of functionality, measuring acceleration in three orthogonal directions, enabling detection of movements in any direction. These accelerometers find applications in navigation systems, image stabilization, and automotive safety systems.</p></p>
